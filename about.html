Who is ASAP Rocky?

Rakim Mayers better known as ASAP Rocky is an American Rapper, songwriter, record producer, and actor. He was born on October 3 ,1988, in Harlem. He learned how to rap from his older brother, who was later killed. The death inspired Rocky to take rapping more seriously. Due to taking music seriously at such a young age, in 2007 he joined the ASAP Mob. While being with the ASAP Mob he released singles which became very popular and quickly received attention from record labels. Since then he has created many singles and albums such as "Long.Live.ASAP" "At. Long. Last. A$AP" "Live.Long.ASAP" and recently released "Testing". Beyond from rapping, ASAP Rocky has model for high branded clothing companies. He has been in magazines covers such as Vouge.
